Workouts in Day 2

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., jumping rope, brisk walking)
  • Wide Grip Push-Ups:  3 sets of 10 reps
  • Chin-Ups (Assisted or Regular): 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Diamond Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dips (Assisted or Regular): 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Inverted Rows (using a sturdy table or bar at waist height): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 20 taps (10 per side)

Day 2

Our Upper Body Calisthenics Sculpt is designed to target and tone your upper body, helping you build lean muscle, improve posture, and increase upper body strength. By incorporating exercises such as wide grip push-ups, chin-ups, diamond push-ups, and dips, you engage your chest, back, shoulders, and arms effectively.

These bodyweight exercises challenge your muscles from different angles, stimulating growth and development. Inverted rows work your back muscles, enhancing posture and reducing the risk of back pain. Plank shoulder taps engage your core and shoulder stabilizers, fostering better coordination and balance. This workout not only sculpts your upper body but also contributes to improved functional strength, making daily tasks easier and more enjoyable.
