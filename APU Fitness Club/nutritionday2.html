Day 2: Protein Power

Nutrition Plans

A well-balanced nutrition plan is essential for optimizing your fitness results and overall well-being. Our 5-Day Nutrition Plan provides a variety of nutritious and delicious meals that support your fitness goals and keep you energized throughout the week. Remember to customize portion sizes based on your individual needs and consult a healthcare professional if you have any specific dietary concerns.

Meals in Day 2

Foods can alternate between all 5 days
    Breakfast:
  • Protein smoothie (blend banana, almond milk, protein powder, and spinach)
  • Whole-grain granola bar

    • Lunch:
  • Turkey and hummus wrap with whole-grain tortilla, lettuce, and tomatoes
  • Carrot sticks with hummus

    • Snack:
  • Cottage cheese with sliced peaches

    • Dinner:
  • Grilled shrimp with garlic and herbs
  • Quinoa pilaf with mixed vegetables

Day 2

Day 2 revolves around protein power, ensuring your muscles receive the support they need. The protein smoothie kickstarts your day with a muscle-repairing blend. The turkey and hummus wrap boasts lean protein with a punch of flavor from hummus. Cottage cheese serves as a high-protein snack, and grilled shrimp takes center stage at dinner, providing a delectable protein source. Quinoa pilaf, rich in protein and nutrients, accompanies the shrimp to create a well-rounded meal.
