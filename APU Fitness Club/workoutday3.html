Workouts in Day 3

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of dynamic stretches
  • Burpees:  3 sets of 12 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Hanging Knee Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Flutter Kicks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Jumping Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg

Day 3

Cardio and Core Calisthenics Burn is an exhilarating workout that elevates your heart rate, burns calories, and strengthens your core muscles. Burpees serve as a full-body exercise, combining cardio, strength, and explosiveness. High knees engage your lower body and cardiovascular system, improving agility and coordination. Russian twists target your obliques and core, enhancing rotational strength. Hanging knee raises challenge your abdominal muscles, improving core stability and endurance. Flutter kicks activate your lower abs and hip flexors, contributing to better lower body strength. Jumping lunges provide a plyometric boost, developing leg power and coordination. This dynamic workout enhances cardiovascular fitness and core strength, making everyday movements more efficient and effortless.
