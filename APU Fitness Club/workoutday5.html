Workouts in Day 5

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., jogging, brisk walking)
  • Foam Rolling: Spend 10-15 minutes targeting major muscle groups
  • Yoga or Stretching Routine: 30 minutes of gentle stretching and deep breathing exercises

Day 5

Active Recovery & Flexibility is all about rejuvenating your body and mind, enhancing flexibility, and promoting overall well-being. Foam rolling helps release muscle tension and knots, providing relief from tight muscles and aiding in muscle recovery. The gentle stretching and deep breathing exercises of yoga or a stretching routine promote relaxation and reduce stress, enhancing your mental well-being. This active recovery day allows your body to repair and regenerate, supporting better overall performance in subsequent workouts. By focusing on flexibility, you improve joint mobility and prevent injuries, ensuring that your fitness journey remains safe and sustainable. Take this day to unwind and reset, allowing your body to thrive in the days to come.
