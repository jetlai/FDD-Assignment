Day 4: Energizing Carbs

Nutrition Plans

A well-balanced nutrition plan is essential for optimizing your fitness results and overall well-being. Our 5-Day Nutrition Plan provides a variety of nutritious and delicious meals that support your fitness goals and keep you energized throughout the week. Remember to customize portion sizes based on your individual needs and consult a healthcare professional if you have any specific dietary concerns.

Meals in Day 4

Foods can alternate between all 5 days
    Breakfast:
  • Whole-grain pancakes with sliced bananas and a drizzle of maple syrup

    • Lunch:
  • Grilled vegetable and feta cheese wrap with whole-wheat tortilla
  • Mixed green salad with olive oil and lemon dressing

    • Snack:
  • Apple slices with peanut butter

    • Dinner:
  • Whole-grain pasta with marinara sauce, lean ground turkey, and sautéed spinach

Day 4

On Day 4, energizing carbohydrates take the spotlight. Whole-grain pancakes offer fiber and sustained energy, and the grilled vegetable and feta cheese wrap presents a satisfying lunch option. The snack features apple slices paired with peanut butter, which adds protein and healthy fats. The dinner incorporates whole-grain pasta with marinara sauce, a tasty dish providing carbohydrates to replenish glycogen stores and support recovery.
