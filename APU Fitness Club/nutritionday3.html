Day 3: Plant-Based Delight

Nutrition Plans

A well-balanced nutrition plan is essential for optimizing your fitness results and overall well-being. Our 5-Day Nutrition Plan provides a variety of nutritious and delicious meals that support your fitness goals and keep you energized throughout the week. Remember to customize portion sizes based on your individual needs and consult a healthcare professional if you have any specific dietary concerns.

Meals in Day 3

Foods can alternate between all 5 days
    Breakfast:
  • Overnight oats with almond milk, chia seeds, and mixed berries

    • Lunch:
  • Chickpea salad with diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese
  • Lemon-tahini dressing

    • Snack:
  • Sliced bell peppers with guacamole

    • Dinner:
  • Stir-fried tofu with broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas
  • Brown rice on the side

Day 3

Embrace plant-based nutrition on Day 3 with a focus on wholesome ingredients. Overnight oats offer fiber and nutrients, while the chickpea salad adds plant-based protein, fiber, and a range of vitamins and minerals. Snack on bell peppers with guacamole, providing healthy fats and vitamin C. The dinner features a stir-fried tofu, rich in plant-based protein and calcium, alongside brown rice, offering complex carbohydrates for energy.
