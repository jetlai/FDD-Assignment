Workout Plans

Welcome to the Workout Plans page! Here, you'll find a variety of carefully curated workout routines designed to target different muscle groups, improve overall strength, and enhance your fitness level. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these workout plans are adaptable to suit your needs. Remember to consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new exercise regimen.

Workouts in Day 1

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g. jumping jacks, high knees)
  • Pull-Ups (Assisted or Regular):  3 sets of 8 reps
  • Push-Ups (Regular or Knee Push-Ups): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank to Push-Up: 3 sets of 10 reps (start in a plank, move to push-up position and back to plank)
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 1

Kickstart your week with our Full-Body Calisthenics Blast, an invigorating workout that engages multiple muscle groups, boosts cardiovascular endurance, and enhances overall body wellness. Calisthenics exercises utilize your body's weight as resistance, helping you develop functional strength and flexibility. Pull-ups strengthen your back and upper body, while push-ups target your chest, shoulders, and arms. Bodyweight squats work your lower body, improving leg strength and stability.

Plank to push-up exercise challenges your core and upper body simultaneously, promoting better balance and stability. Bulgarian split squats focus on unilateral strength, reducing muscle imbalances and preventing injuries. Mountain climbers provide a fantastic cardio boost, elevating your heart rate and burning calories. This full-body workout will leave you feeling empowered and energized, setting a positive tone for the rest of the week.
