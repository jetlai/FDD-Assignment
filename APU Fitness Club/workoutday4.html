Workouts in Day 4

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., jogging, brisk walking)
  • Pistol Squats (Assisted or Regular):  3 sets of 6 reps per leg
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Box Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Wall Sits:  3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 4

The Lower Body Calisthenics Power workout is specifically designed to strengthen and tone your lower body, including your legs, glutes, and calves. Pistol squats are a powerful exercise that challenges your leg strength, balance, and flexibility. Glute bridges target your glutes and lower back, enhancing hip stability and reducing the risk of lower back pain. Calf raises focus on your calf muscles, promoting better ankle stability and preventing injuries. Walking lunges engage your quadriceps and glutes, improving lower body strength and balance. Box jumps are an explosive exercise that boosts lower body power and coordination. Wall sits are a static hold that targets your quadriceps, promoting better knee stability and strength. This workout contributes to improved lower body endurance, making daily activities like walking and climbing stairs more effortless and enjoyable.
